Let's Learn About Colors!

Purple: Purple is the color of royalty and magic. It's made by mixing red and blue!
Yellow: Yellow is the color of sunshine and happiness. It's bright and cheerful!
Blue: Blue is the color of the sky and the ocean. It's calming and cool!
Red: Red is the color of fire and love. It's bold and exciting!
Black: Black is the color of night and mystery. It's strong and powerful!
Green: Green is the color of nature and plants. It's fresh and peaceful!
